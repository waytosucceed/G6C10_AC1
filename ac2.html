Adverbs and their types.


Overview of the Grammar topics:

  • In a sentence, Adverbs of degree tell us the extent to which an action happens. They also modify the extent of a certain quality in a noun or pronoun.

  • In a sentence, Conjunctive Adverbs, join clauses together, forming relations between expressions such as addition, contrast, and order.

  • In a sentence, Relative Adverbs, join a relative clause to another sentence or independent clause.

  • As their name suggests, Adverbs of Direction, tell us the direction of an action.
